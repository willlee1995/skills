## Notes on key clauses

- **Deposit non-refundable**: it reserves capacity you turn other work away for. State this plainly.
- **Balance before handover**: never release final files before final payment.
- **Kill fee**: protects against cancellation mid-project; 25-50% is standard.
- **Revision definition**: always define a "round" in the quote, not just the contract — it prevents the most common dispute.
- **Validity window**: 30 days stops a client booking at last year's rate.
