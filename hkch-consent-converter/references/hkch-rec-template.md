# HKCH REC Document Template Structure

The HKCH REC informed consent document has 4 parts in this exact order. Each part starts on the same page flow (no forced page breaks between parts in the reference).

## Part 1: 參與研究資料頁 (Information Sheet — Chinese)

### Section Order and Content

1. **研究題目** (center, bold) — Full study title in Chinese
2. **參與研究資料頁** (center, bold) — subtitle: `(適用於參加者/參加者之父母/法定監護人)`
3. **首席研究員** (bold label) — PI name, title, department, HKCH
4. **研究員** (bold label) — Co-investigators list, one per line
5. **研究贊助者** (bold label) — Sponsor name
6. **邀請** (bold heading) — Invitation paragraph (standard boilerplate, adapt pronouns)
7. **研究簡介** (bold heading) — Study-specific introduction/background
8. **研究目的** (bold heading) — Study purpose/objectives
9. **研究程序** (bold heading) — Study procedures in detail
10. **其他程序 / 治療** (bold heading) — Alternative procedures statement
11. **預期研究的持續時間** (bold heading) — Expected study duration
12. **預期的好處** (bold heading) — Expected benefits
13. **潛在的風險 / 不適 / 副作用** (bold heading) — Potential risks
14. **自願參與 / 中途退出** (bold heading) — Voluntary participation (standard boilerplate)
15. **參與研究的費用及報酬** (bold heading) — Costs and payments
16. **賠償和治療** (bold heading) — Compensation/treatment for injury (standard boilerplate)
17. **終止研究** (bold heading) — Study termination (standard boilerplate)
18. **個人資料保密** (bold heading) — Confidentiality (standard boilerplate)
19. Privacy Ordinance paragraph (standard boilerplate — Cap. 486 reference)
20. Authorization list items (numbered, List Paragraph style)
21. **新資訊** (bold heading) — New information (standard boilerplate)
22. **聯絡資料** (bold heading) — Contact information

## Part 2: Information Sheet (English)

Mirror structure of Part 1 in English:

1. **Study Title** (center, bold)
2. **Information Sheet to participate in a research study** (center, bold) — `(For subject / subject's parent / legal guardian)`
3. **Principal Investigator** (bold label)
4. **Co-Investigators** (bold label)
5. **Sponsor** (bold label)
6. **Invitation** (bold heading)
7. **Introduction** (bold heading)
8. **Purpose of the Study** (bold heading)
9. **Study Procedures** (bold heading)
10. **Alternatives Procedures / Treatment** (bold heading)
11. **Expected Duration of Study** (bold heading)
12. **Expected Benefit** (bold heading)
13. **Potential Risks / Discomforts / Side Effects** (bold heading)
14. **Voluntary Participation / Right to Withdraw** (bold heading)
15. **Costs and Payments if participating in the study** (bold heading)
16. **Compensation and Treatment for Injury** (bold heading)
17. **Study Termination** (bold heading)
18. **Confidentiality** (bold heading)
19. Privacy Ordinance paragraph
20. Authorization list
21. **New information** (bold heading)
22. **Contact information** (bold heading)

## Part 3: 知情同意書 (Consent Form — Chinese)

1. **研究題目** (center, bold) — same study title
2. **知情同意書** (center, bold) — `(適用於參加者/參加者之父母/法定監護人)`
3. Consent statement: `本人茲同意本人/本人的孩子參與這項研究。`
4. Understanding statement (read and understand, explained, benefits/risks, questions answered, sufficient information)
5. Compensation statement (treatment for discomfort, no legal rights waived)
6. Withdrawal statement (free to withdraw, no impact on care)
7. Confidentiality statement (identity kept confidential, authorize REC and regulatory access)
8. **Signature table** (10 rows — participant, parent/guardian, investigator, consent taker, witness)
9. Copy statement: `簽署同意書後，本人將獲得一份本研究的資料頁及已簽署並註明日期的同意書副本作為保存。`

## Part 4: Informed Consent Form (English)

1. **Study Title** (center, bold)
2. **Informed Consent Form** (center, bold) — `(For subject / subject's parent / legal guardian)`
3. Consent statement: `I hereby consent to participant / consent my child to participate in the research study.`
4. Understanding statement
5. Compensation statement
6. Withdrawal statement
7. Confidentiality statement
8. **Signature tables** (two tables: 6 rows + 4 rows)
9. Copy statement

## Standard Boilerplate Text

The following sections use standardized text that should be reused across all HKCH REC documents (adapt only study-specific placeholders like [PI name], [phone number], [REC name]):

### Invitation (Chinese)
> 我們誠邀您/您的孩子參與本研究。在您決定是否參與/是否讓您的孩子參與之前，請您了解進行研究的原因和研究所涉及的內容，了解這些資訊是很重要的。請花時間仔細閱讀下列資訊，若您願意，請與家人、朋友及您/您的孩子的家庭醫生討論。若有任何不清楚的地方，或若您希望獲取更多資訊，請向研究人員查詢。請花時間決定您是否參與/是否讓您的孩子參與本研究。

### Invitation (English)
> You are / your child is being invited to participate in the research. Before you decide whether you / your child should take part, it is important for you to understand why the research is being done and what it will involve. Please take time to read the following information carefully and, if you wish, discuss it with family, friends, and your / your child's family doctor. Ask research investigators if there is anything that is not clear or if you wish to obtain more information. Please take time to decide whether you will participate / you will let your child participate in the research.

### Voluntary Participation (Chinese)
> 您/您的孩子參與本研究純屬自願性質。在任何時候，您和您的孩子有權拒絕參與或隨時中途退出本研究，而無需提供理由。這將不會影響您/您的孩子現在及日後的常規醫療護理和服務。一旦您和您的孩子決定退出本研究，如果沒有提出特別要求銷毀退出前所收集有關您/您的孩子的數據，我們將會繼續使用。您和您的孩子會被給予足夠的時間去考慮是否參與本研究。簽署同意書後，您將獲得一份參與研究資料頁及已簽署並註明日期的同意書副本作為紀錄。

### Voluntary Participation (English)
> Your / your child's participation in this study is voluntary. You and your child may refuse to participate or withdraw from the study at any time without giving any reason. It will not affect your/your child's present and future medical treatments and services. If you and your child decided to withdraw from the study, we will continue to handle the data we collected before your/your child's withdrawal unless we received your request to discard all your/your child's study-related information on this study. You and your child will have enough time for considering the participation in this study. By signing a written consent form, you will be given a participant information sheet and a signed and dated copy of the consent form for record.

### Compensation (Chinese)
> 若您/您的孩子因參與本研究而引致任何身體不適或情緒上的波動，研究人員將會為您/您的孩子進行治療或轉介您/您的孩子接受治療。簽署本知情同意書並不表示您/您的孩子放棄任何法律權利。

### Compensation (English)
> If you have / your child has any physical or emotional discomfort as a result of participating in this study, the research investigators will treat you / your child, or will refer you / your child to receive treatment. Signing this informed consent form does not imply that your / your child's legal rights would be waived.

### Study Termination (Chinese)
> 若出現任何未預料到的情況或研究人員決定終止研究以符合您/您的孩子的最佳利益，研究人員有權隨時終止研究。

### Study Termination (English)
> When there is an occurrence of any unexpected situation or research investigators decide it is in your/ your child's best interest to stop the study, research investigators have the right to terminate the study.

### Confidentiality (Chinese)
> 所有在本研究中收集的可識別您/您的孩子身份的資料將會按法律規定得到保密處理及不會公開。可識別您/您的孩子身份的資料亦不會出現於任何與本研究相關的公開發表報告。所有資料亦會在研究完成後十年銷毀。

### Confidentiality (English)
> The records identifying the subject will be kept confidential and, to the extent permitted by the applicable laws and/or regulations, will not be made publicly available. If the results of the trial are published, the subject's identity will remain confidential. All data will be destroyed ten years after the completion of the study.

### Privacy Ordinance (Chinese)
> 根據香港法律規定（特別是第 486 章《個人資料（私隱）條例》），您有對您/您的孩子的個人資料保密的權利，例如在本研究中或與本研究有關的個人資料的收集、監管、保留、管理、控制、使用（包括分析或比較）、轉進或轉出香港、不披露、清除和／或以任何方式處理或棄置的權利。如有任何問題，請您諮詢個人資料私隱專員或其職員（電話號碼：2827 2827），以瞭解妥善監控或監管您/您的孩子的個人資料保護之事宜，以確保您完整掌握和瞭解遵守規管個人資料私隱的法律之重要性。

### Privacy Ordinance (English)
> Under the laws of Hong Kong (in particular the Personal Data (Privacy) Ordinance (Cap. 486), you shall have the right for the protection of your/your child's personal data, such as those regarding the collection, custody, retention, management, control, use (including analysis or comparison), transfer in or out of Hong Kong, non-disclosure, erasure and/or in any way dealing with or disposing of any of your/your child's personal data in or for this study. For any query, you should consult the Privacy Commissioner for Personal Data or his office (Tel No. 2827-2827) as to the proper monitoring or supervision of your/your child's personal data protection so that your full awareness and understanding of the significance of compliance with the law governing privacy data is assured.

### Authorization Items (Chinese)
> 同意參與本研究，您明確作出以下授權：
> 1. 研究計劃負責人、其研究團隊和香港兒童醫院研究倫理委員會根據本項研究和本同意書之規定監督存取、使用並保存您/您的孩子的個人資料；
> 2. 相關的政府機構（如香港衛生署、醫院管理局）可獲得您/您的孩子的個人資料以檢查和核實研究資料的完整性，以及評估研究協定與相關要求的一致性。

### Authorization Items (English)
> By consenting to participate in this study, you expressly authorize:
> 1. the principal investigator, his/her research team and Hong Kong Children's Hospital Research Ethics Committee are responsible for overseeing this study to get access to, to use, and to retain your/your child's personal data for the purposes and in the manner described in this informed consent process; and
> 2. the relevant government agencies (e.g. the Hong Kong Department of Health, Hospital Authority) to get access to your/your child's personal data for the purposes of checking and verifying the integrity of study data and assessing compliance with the study protocol and relevant requirements.

### New Information (Chinese)
> 如出現有關本研究的任何新資訊，而這些資訊會影響您是否繼續參與/您是否讓您的孩子繼續參與研究的決定，您將及時獲得通知。您可能需要簽署新的知情同意書，以表示您已獲知會有關本研究的新資訊。

### New Information (English)
> If any new information about the research study becomes available which may influence your decision for considering the participation / the participation of your child in this study, you will be told in a timely manner. You may be asked to sign a new consent form if new information relating to this study becomes available.

### Contact Information (Chinese) — template
> 如有任何有關本研究的查詢，您可以致電 [PHONE] 與 [PI_NAME_ZH] 聯絡。若您對您/您的孩子作為研究參加者所享有的權利有任何疑問，您可致電3513 3193與醫管局中央研究倫理委員會聯絡。

### Contact Information (English) — template
> If you have any questions about the research, please feel free to contact [PI_NAME_EN] at [PHONE]. If you have questions related to your/your child's rights as a research participant, please contact Hospital Authority Central Institutional Review Board at 3513 3193.

### Consent Statements (Chinese)
> 本人茲同意本人/本人的孩子參與這項研究。
>
> 本人已閱讀並了解這份知情同意書。研究人員已向本人詳細解釋研究的細節。本人明白所有有關本研究的好處及風險。本人有機會向研究人員提出疑問，而他/她亦已完滿地解答本人的疑問。對於此研究，本人已獲得足夠的資料。
>
> 若本人/本人的孩子因參與本研究而引致任何身體不適或情緒上的波動，研究人員將會為本人/本人的孩子進行治療或轉介本人/本人的孩子接受治療。簽署本知情同意書並不表示本人/本人的孩子放棄任何法律權利。
>
> 本人明白，本人/本人的孩子可隨時自由退出本研究。本人明白，假如本人/本人的孩子選擇不參與或退出研究，本人/本人的孩子現在及日後所接受的醫療護理將不受此決定影響。
>
> 本人明白，本人/本人的孩子之身份將獲得保密處理。本人亦允許研究倫理委員會及有關法定機構在合適的條例及法例容許下及在不侵犯本人/本人的孩子的私隱情況中，直接翻查本人/本人的孩子的研究數據以核實有關的臨床研究資料。

### Consent Statements (English)
> I hereby consent to participant / consent my child to participate in the research study.
>
> I have read and understand this document. The study has been explained to me. I understand all the benefits and risks related to this study. I have opportunities to raise questions to the investigators and they have answered my questions to my satisfaction. I have collected sufficient information regarding this study.
>
> If I have / my child has any physical or emotional discomfort as a result of participating in this study, the study investigators will treat me / my child, or will refer me / my child to receive treatment. Signing this informed consent form does not imply that my / my child's legal rights would be waived.
>
> I understand that I can freely withdraw my / my child's consent on the participation of this study and it would not affect my / my child's present and future medical care.
>
> I understand my / my child's identity will be kept confidential. I also authorize the research ethics committee and the regulatory authority(ies) to access my / my child's data for verification of clinical trial procedures and/or data, without violating my / my child's confidentiality, to the extent permitted by the applicable laws and regulations.

### Copy Statement (Chinese)
> 簽署同意書後，本人將獲得一份本研究的資料頁及已簽署並註明日期的同意書副本作為保存。

### Copy Statement (English)
> By signing a written informed consent form, I will be given a signed and dated copy of the consent form and information sheet for storage.
