# Countdown Video

Make a countdown that ends exactly when it should. The whole craft is one idea: derive the remaining time from the frame (or a monotonic timestamp), then format and animate that number — never count down with a `setInterval`. Covers livestream "starting soon" screens, launch and flash-sale countdowns, and looping backgrounds. For creators who need a timer that lands on zero on the exact frame.

- "Animate a mm:ss or dd:hh:mm:ss timer."
